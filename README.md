# Jeewoong Derm Lab

**Dermatology · Skin Microbiome · Epidemiology · Bioinformatics**

Jeewoong Derm Lab develops computational workflows and conducts translational research at the intersection of dermatology, microbiome science, and population health. By integrating nationwide clinical datasets, skin microbiome sequencing, and epidemiologic methods, we investigate the pathogenesis, diagnosis, and treatment of skin disease from bench to population scale.

---

## Research Focus

Our laboratory investigates:

- Skin cancer epidemiology and outcomes
- Skin microbiome and shotgun metagenomics
- Host–microbiome interactions in cutaneous disease
- Nationwide population-based cohort studies
- Dermatologic surgery and reconstructive techniques
- Hair and nail disorders
