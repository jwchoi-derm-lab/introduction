# 🧬 Welcome to Jeewoong's Derm Lab

**Dermatology | Skin Microbiome | Epidemiology | Bioinformatics**

Welcome! This GitHub serves as the official repository for the **Jeewoong Derm Lab**, where we develop reproducible computational workflows and conduct translational research in dermatology. Our work integrates nationwide clinical datasets, skin microbiome sequencing, epidemiology, and bioinformatics to better understand the pathogenesis, diagnosis, and treatment of skin diseases.

---

# 🔬 Research Interests

Our laboratory focuses on:

- 🦠 Skin microbiome and shotgun metagenomics
- 🧬 Host–microbiome interactions in skin diseases
- 📊 Nationwide population-based cohort studies
- 💻 Bioinformatics and reproducible computational pipelines
- 🔪 Dermatologic surgery
- 🎗️ Skin cancer
- 💇 Hair and nail disorders
